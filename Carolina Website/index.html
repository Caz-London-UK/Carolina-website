Scaling Global Staff-Augmentation
Reducing "Time-to-Land" from 6 to 3 weeks
with a 45-candidate global pipeline

In a global staff-aug model, a 6-week "Time-to-Land" created significant revenue risk. The business required a system to respond to client demands in days, not weeks, without compromising technical rigour.

6→3
weeks · time-to-land
01
Strategic Inventory Modelling
Action
Moved from reactive hiring to a "Strategic Inventory" model, calculating a 45-candidate global bench to absorb 25% monthly churn.
Result
Established a predictable, data-driven roadmap for sourcing that anticipated regional demand spikes before they occurred.
02
Technical Practice Integration
Action

Result
Created a pool of pre-cleared talent, allowing the business to present vetted profiles to clients within 48 hours of a request.
03
Operational Orchestration
Action
Scaled the process by coaching a Team Assistant to manage high-volume availability checks and time-zone scheduling.

Eliminated pipeline decay and administrative bottlenecks, ensuring the bench remained 100% actionable 24/7.

2016 – 2021

Transforming Hiring Economics
From "Growth Tax" to £300k+ Annual Savings

Upon joining a 120-person boutique agency, I identified that a 20% agency fee model was becoming a growth tax. With 15–20% attrition and rising senior salaries, the business was spending heavily on reactive replacements rather than strategic growth.

£300k+
annual savings
01
Commercial Negotiation (The Quick Win)
Action
Negotiated standard vendor terms from 20% down to 15%.
Result
£45k saved in Year 1, establishing immediate credibility and proving that Talent Operations could actively protect the P&L.
02
Building Internal Engines (The Cultural Shift)
Action
Revived a stagnant internal referral programme and pivoted to organic, network-led sourcing with a minimal budget.
Result
Generated 28 leads and a 25% conversion rate, moving the culture away from agency-first thinking.
03
Scaling the Direct Model (The Ownership)
Action
As headcount grew to 150+ and attrition reached 20%, I fully embedded a direct-sourcing model as a sole practitioner.
Result
Eliminated agency reliance for the majority of roles, supporting 30+ hires annually.
04
The Bottom Line: Strategic Value Driver
Financial Impact
Delivered £300k+ in annual cost avoidance by replacing high-fee agencies with internal pipelines.
Operational Outcome
Enhanced cultural durability and candidate quality by leveraging internal referrals and organic network — ensuring hires were aligned with the agency's boutique values.
Organisational Shift
The success of this model was the catalyst for leadership to restructure my role into a 100% dedicated Talent Strategy function, moving from HR support to strategic growth ownership.
Reflection
I traded the safe, reactive model for the curiosity of a builder, proving that staying open to new ways of working can transform a company's economics.
